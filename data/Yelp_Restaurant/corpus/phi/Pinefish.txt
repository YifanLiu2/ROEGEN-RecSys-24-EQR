Go.I came here during restaurant week for my mom's birthday. We had a reservation and didn't have to wait long. The food was good, way better then I thought it would be. I got the shrimp with was delicious and very garlicy. The crab cakes were also delicious, most meat. For dessert half the table got carrot cake, the other half chocolate cake, both were good. The only thing we had that wasn't good was the fried cauliflower. I've heard good things about it and also mixed reviews. There was way to much of a vinegar taste on it. I was only able to have a bite. Overall the decor was nice and the waitress was very attentive.My friends and I came for brunch and the food was delicious! The restaurant is also super cute in the inside and I loved the vibe of the restaurant.
